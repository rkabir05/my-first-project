THE

BEGINNING

I embarked on my journey into the leather industry quite unexpectedly. It all began when I crafted a simple leather guitar strap for myself, driven by the desire for a personalized and durable accessory. The satisfaction of creating something with my own hands sparked an unexpected passion.

A COMMITMENT

TO CRAFT

Friends and fellow musicians admired the strap, prompting requests for custom pieces. Recognizing the potential, I decided to delve deeper into the craft, mastering various techniques and sourcing high-quality materials.

FAST FORWARD

WHERE WE ARE TODAY

WP is now a small-batch maker of quality leather goods built for the long haul. We make each piece in our small workshop in Leon, Mexico.
